The lyrical god of desi hip hop

Krishna Kaul, known mononymously as Krishna (stylised now as KR$NA; previously as Krsna; known formerly as YoungProzpekt) is an Indian rapper .He was one of the earliest rappers to emerge in the Indian hip hop scene in the mid-2000s under the stage name Prozpekt. He was briefly featured in the 2019 Bollywood film Gully Boy as himself.He is widely acknowledged within the Desi Hip Hop community for pioneering a lyrical revolution in the genre, infusing a new wave of poetic depth and lyrical complexity into hip hop's narrative.

Biography

  • KRSNA (also known as Krishna Kaul) is a popular Indian rapper and musician known for his contributions to the Desi hip hop music scene.
  • He gained popularity for his conscious and socially relevant lyrics, as well as his unique flow and delivery.
  • KRSNA is particularly known for his ability to mix English and Hindi lyrics in his songs, creating a style that appeals to both Indian and international audiences.
  • He often tackles social and political issues in his music, such as corruption, inequality, and the struggles of the common people.
  • KRSNA has also collaborated with many other Desi hip hop artists, both in India and internationally.
